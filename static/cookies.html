Cookies

In order to track website usage (done by Google Analytics), we use first party cookies.

A cookie is a small text file that a website saves on your computer or mobile device when you visit the site.

Browser localStorage

To store your preferred options between sessions, such as your input code & pane layout, we make use of your browser's localStorage, where we place:

  1. The current page state (Code, selected compilers, pane layout, font sizes)
  2. Your settings as shown in the settings popup (Which can be found at: More > Settings)
